Grapevine closing

Team Grapevine would like to thank all of you who have used Grapevine and those of you who helped make Grapevine a success. Grapevine was the first on the scene to show a new dynamic in person to person, person to group, and group to group communication. It's been a great trip in introducing this new communication paradigm and thanks for joining us for the ride.

We Team Grapevine, have decided it's time to withdraw Grapevine from open usage and move on to the next chapter. Don't worry - if something cool comes up, we will be sure to get ahold of you!

If you do want to contact us help@grapevinetalk.com is still active.

Thanks for all the fish,

Team Grapevine
